Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is EarlyBirds?

EarlyBirds is your platform for convenience in booking air flights which can go as close as within the same day for flight time, in few hours time before departure.

How do I get started in using EarlyBirds?

To start using our services, you will have to register for a user account.
To get started, click on "MAKE ME AN EARLY BIRD!" from the main page, and you will be redirected to the registration page.
You can proceed to click on "SIGN IN" button if you already are a user of Early Birds.

I have been redirected to the profile page after registraton is complete. Should I complete filling up incomplete information?

It is recommended to complete filling up the profile information, as it gives us convenience to contact you if necessary.
Your personal information is private and will not be shared to third parties.

How do I get started with booking at ticket after I have completed my profile?

To get started, be sure to click on "Main Menu" button, to be redirected to the main booking page, where users can start searching for flight booking!

How do I search for available flight?

At the "Main Menu", start by searching for name of "Departure Airport" as well as "Arrival Airport".
Then, select your flight "Class", and set the specified "Departure" Date.
When the information is complete, click on "SEARCH", and it will filter the flight booking results relevant to your search.

I have found and selected my desired flight booking, what do I need to fill up?

Upon selecton of desired flight booking, complete fillung up your contact details, as ir is required for the delivery of the e-ticket.
Users are also required to fill up passenger details according to the number of passengers within the flight booking.

What payment methods do you accept when making bookings from EarlyBirds?

Payment methods accepted are as follows:

-Credit/Debit Card
-Online Banking
-e-Wallet

Please ensure that you have sufficient balance from your selected payment method to avoid disruption of the payment during the booking process.

I have completed my payment for my flight booking, how do I review my completed order?

Upon completion of payment, you will be shows a review of your e-ticket flight booking, and it will also be saved to your booking history.
To check on your booking history, you can visit your profile by clicking on "PROFILE" and it will be shown below your personal information section.
